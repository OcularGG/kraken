Naval Pyramid

Sea Lords

CHOSEN

First Sea Lord

Supreme Commander of the KRAKEN Fleet. Responsible for all naval operations, strategic command decisions, and overall fleet leadership. Reports directly to no one and has ultimate authority over all naval matters.

WarLord4082

Second Sea Lord

Deputy Commander responsible for fleet operations and tactical coordination across all naval theaters. Serves as the primary lieutenant to the First Sea Lord and manages day-to-day operational activities.

Magoo

Third Sea Lord

Naval Personnel and Administrative Command. Oversees recruitment, training, personnel management, and ensures the fleet maintains proper staffing and skill development across all ranks.

Kpt. Gneisenau

Fourth Sea Lord

Logistics and Supply Chain Command. Manages fleet resources, equipment procurement, maintenance schedules, and ensures all ships are properly supplied for extended operations.

Kpt. Scharnhorst

Fifth Sea Lord

Strategic Planning and Intelligence Command. Coordinates long-term strategy, intelligence operations, enemy analysis, and develops tactical doctrines for the fleet.

Admirals

Tommy Templeman

Admiral of the Fleet

Fleet Command and Operations. Direct command of all fleet movements, tactical operations, and battle coordination. Leads the fleet in major engagements and oversees all naval combat operations.

Admiral Johnson

Admiral

Naval Operations Command. Oversees major fleet operations, strategic deployments, and coordinates with other admirals to ensure effective fleet management and tactical execution.

Admiral Smith

Admiral

Fleet Strategy Command. Develops and implements strategic naval doctrine, fleet tactics, and long-term operational planning. Works closely with intelligence to adapt strategies.

R. Adm. Brown

Rear Admiral

Squadron Command. Leads squadron operations and tactical formations in naval engagements. Commands multiple ships in coordinated attacks and defensive maneuvers.

R. Adm. Davis

Rear Admiral

Tactical Operations. Specializes in combat tactics, battlefield coordination, and real-time tactical decision making during naval conflicts and strategic operations.

R. Adm. Wilson

Rear Admiral

Intelligence Operations. Manages naval intelligence gathering, reconnaissance missions, enemy analysis, and provides critical information for strategic planning.
